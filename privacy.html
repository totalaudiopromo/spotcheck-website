Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 December 2025

Spot Check ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally on your device using browser storage and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Playlist validation history and results
  • Your usage count (for free tier limits)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Tracked playlist data

Data Transmitted to Our Servers

If you upgrade to a paid subscription:

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • Subscription status (plan type, expiry date)
  • Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide playlist validation services
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Improve our extension and services
  • Send important service updates (if subscribed)

Third-Party Services

Spot Check integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Spotify API - To fetch playlist data. We only access publicly available playlist information. We do not access your Spotify account or listening history.
  • Stripe - For secure payment processing. Your payment details are handled directly by Stripe and never touch our servers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local storage data remains on your device
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your data
  • Delete - Clear all local data via extension settings
  • Cancel - Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Withdraw consent - Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection

Cookies

Spot Check does not use cookies. All data is stored using browser extension storage APIs.

Children's Privacy

Spot Check is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
