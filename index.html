Frequently asked questions

Is Spot Check safe to use?

Absolutely. Spot Check only accesses publicly available Spotify playlist data. We never access your personal Spotify account, listening history, or private playlists. All data is processed securely, and we never sell your information to third parties.

How does playlist validation work?

When you visit a Spotify playlist page, Spot Check analyses the playlist's public metrics including follower count, track history, and curator activity. Our algorithm compares these against patterns typically seen in bot-inflated playlists to calculate a risk score.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked, no hidden fees, no hassle.

What data do you access?

We only access publicly available playlist information: playlist name, description, follower count, track list, and curator details. We do not access your Spotify login, personal playlists, or any private account data.
