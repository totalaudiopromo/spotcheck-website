## Long Description

### Overview
Stop wasting money on fake playlists. Spot Check validates Spotify playlists instantly, helping artists and music promoters identify bot-inflated playlists before pitching.

### Key Features

**Bot Detection**
Our algorithm analyses follower patterns, growth spikes, and engagement metrics to identify fake playlists. Get a clear risk score for every playlist you check.

**Instant Validation**
Simply browse to any Spotify playlist page and Spot Check automatically displays key metrics including follower count, track count, and curator information.

**Curator Verification**
View curator profiles, check their other playlists, and verify their legitimacy before pitching your music.

**Follower Trends (Premium)**
Track playlist performance over time. Spot suspicious growth patterns and monitor changes after your track is added.

**Bulk Validation (Premium)**
Check up to 50 playlists at once. Perfect for validating entire pitch lists before a campaign.

**Export Reports (Premium)**
Download validation results as CSV or JSON. Keep records for your clients or your own campaigns.

**Change Alerts (Premium)**
Get notified when tracked playlists change. Know immediately if your track is removed or followers drop.

### Pricing
- **Free**: 5 checks per day, basic metrics, curator info
- **Premium**: Unlimited checks, bulk validation, historical tracking, bot detection score, export

### Privacy First
Spot Check only accesses publicly available playlist data. We never access your personal Spotify account, listening history, or private playlists. Your data stays on your device.

### Built by Music Promoters
Created by Total Audio Promo, with 5+ years of experience in radio and playlist promotion. We built the tool we wished existed.
